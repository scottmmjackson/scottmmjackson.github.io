The following demo parses a first-order differential equation and approximates it with Euler's Method

Equations should be in the form of a javascript expression, e.g.: Math.cos(t) for cos(t), Math.pow(Math.E,2*y) for e2y. See Math Library. Syntax errors will be reported in the output window.
